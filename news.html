November 2024

In November, the PLACID instrument and the corresponding crates were successfully delivered to the summit before the heavy snowfall period - before the accessibility of the roads is no longer guaranteed. Furthermore, the KORAY derotator required internal realignment as well as on-sky alignment with the mechanical axis of the telescope. The delicate process of removing and inserting the derotator into the telescope on the Nasmyth platform can be seen on the images. This process has been successfully completed and the enclosure of the Nasmyth platform is currently being installed (see black and yellow structure). As a little teaser, you can see the night sky photographed next to the observatory.
