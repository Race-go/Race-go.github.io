November 2024: KORAY installation and PLACID delivery to the summit

In November, the PLACID instrument and the corresponding crates were successfully delivered to the summit before the heavy snowfall period - before the accessibility of the roads is no longer guaranteed. The last section of the road to the observatory and around the DAG itself have been paved (compare to images from September 2024).

Furthermore, the KORAY derotator required internal realignment as well as on-sky alignment with the mechanical axis of the telescope. The delicate process of removing and inserting the derotator into the telescope on the Nasmyth platform can be seen on the images. This process has been successfully completed and the enclosure of the Nasmyth platform is currently being installed (see black and yellow structure).

September 2024: KORAY alignment and mounting

Upgraded facilites at the DAG. In-lab alignment of the KORAY derotator and consequent pre-installation of KORAY into the telescope flange.
