Research

1) PLACID Commissioning and Early Science

The PLACID (Polarization Light Adaptive Coronagraphic Imager Development) project is focused on the commissioning and early science phases of its development. This section outlines the objectives, challenges, and preliminary results of the PLACID project as it begins to take shape.

2) Understanding Discrete Pixelated Phase Coronagraphy with SLMs

This section delves into the fundamentals of discrete pixelated phase coronagraphy using Spatial Light Modulators (SLMs). The focus is on understanding the mechanics, applications, and advantages of this technology in high-contrast imaging systems.

3) Coherent Differential Imaging (CDI)

Coherent Differential Imaging (CDI) is a cutting-edge technique used to enhance image contrast and suppress noise in astronomical observations. This section explores the principles behind CDI, its implementation, and its potential impact on observational astronomy.
